About the Project

This is a website that creates a clean sharable image with descriptors for whatever your heart desires.

Project Details

  • Languages: Javascript, CSS, and HTML
  • Functionality: Creates a container with descriptors for any image.

I have always enjoyed the way Spotify's app creates an image for a social media story for any song you'd like to share. I took a look at this and thought, why not anything being promoted? So I went ahead and made this!
